What is Californian values?

Californian values is a modification of the popular “8values” quiz that maps out what your political values would be if you were a citizen of the New California Republic. You’ll be presented with a series of statements you’ll be prompted to answer from “Strongly agree” to “Strongly disagree” at the end you’ll be given a score along each axis and a matching ideology.

What are Californian values?

There are four independent axes - Economy, Intergration, Foreign Policy, and Utopia - and each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are:

Homesteading

Those with higher homesteading scores believe by protecting small farmers we can build a robust middle class and a fairer economy. At higher values they may believe in land reform to give sharecroppers control over the land they farm.

ECONOMIC

Baronism

Those with higher Baronism scores believe the free market will unleash the productive capacity of the brahmin baron class to increase the rate of capital accumulation and reindustrialise California.

Desegregation

Those with higher desegregation scores believe in a broad definition of personhood that includes ghouls and supermutants. They believe in equal opportunity for everyone under the NCR’s banner.

INTEGRATION

Human Exceptionalism

Those with higher human exceptionalism scores believe that humans should be separate from ghouls and supermutants given concerns around the risk of ferality, higher capacity for violence and recent history with The Master.

Interventionism

Those with higher interventionism scores believe that the New California Republic should seek to expand out into the wasteland to provide more with the safety, freedom, prosperity and way of life the republic offers. Also to expand our resources.

FOREIGN POLICY

Isolationism

Those with higher isolation scores believe it is best to keep californian business in California. They tend to be skeptikal of threat the legion poses but surely marching into the mojave is the best way to provoke them?

Old World Blues

Those with higher old world blues scores see the NCR as a vehicle for the rebirth of America. They dream of a California filled with beautiful suburbs, shopping malls and fast food.

UTOPIA

New World Hopes

Those with higher new world hopes scores see the end of the world as an opportunity to build something better whatever that may be. America is dead. America remains dead.


What's the "Closest Match" mean at the bottom of the results?

In addition to matching you to the eight values, the quiz also attempts to match you to a political ideology. This is a work in progress and is much less accurate than the values and axes, so don't take it too seriously. If you disagree with your assigned ideology, send us an email at eightvalues@gmail.com with your scores, matched ideology, and preferred ideology, and we'll look into adjusting the system. Thanks!
